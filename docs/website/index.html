Stop getting tracked.

Our mission is to protect your privacy by preventing unauthorized access to your personal data under the guise of PC checks for a game. With "CheckMate," we ensure your files and private information stay secure, so you can enjoy gaming without compromising your right to privacy.

Usage

Simply execute the .bat file, a window saying "Windows protected your PC." may appear, just press on "More Information" and run anyway. Our script is Opensource and checked with VirusTotal.
